# FMCG-Sales-Performance

## Project Overview

This project showcases an interactive Microsoft Excel dashboard developed to analyze FMCG sales performance between 2023 and 2025.
The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into actionable business insights by tracking revenue trends, product performance, regional sales, and profitability.
The objective of this project is to demonstrate data analysis, dashboard development, and business reporting skills commonly required in Sales Analyst and Commercial Analyst roles within the FMCG industry.

## Business Case

An FMCG company distributes hundreds of products across multiple countries and sales channels. Management needs a dashboard to answer key business questions, including:
1. How has revenue changed over time?
2. Which product categories contribute the most to sales?
3. Which products generate the highest profit?
4. Which countries perform the best?
5. Which products contribute most to overall profit?

## Objectives

The dashboard was developed to:
1. Monitor sales performance
2. Analyze category contribution
3. Identify top-performing products
4. Evaluate regional performance
5. Perform Pareto analysis

## Dashboard Features

### KPI Summary
Net Revenue
Quantity Sold
Gross Profit
YoY Growth
### Sales Trend
Monitor monthly revenue trends from 2023–2025.
### Country Performance
Compare sales performance across countries.
### Product Performance
Identify products generating the highest profit.
### Sales Performance by Category
Visualize performance of each product category.
### Pareto Analysis
Identify high-impact products contributing most of the company's profit.
